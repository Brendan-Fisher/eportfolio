About

I am currently studying Computer Science and pursuing a minor in Mathematics. Many of my classes focus on AI and Machine Learning fundamentals as these are topics I am interested in. Many of the classes I have taken have culminated in a group project, usually consisting of designing and developing a web app. It is through these classes and personal projects that I have gained many of the developing skills that I currently possess.

During Summer 2021, I was afforded the opportunity to do research as a part of the Engaging Learning Lab at the University of Florida under the direction of Dr. Christina Gardner-McCune. During my time working with her and the team, I developed AI and machine learning curriculum for a high school level summer camp in Orlando as well as designed activities and content directed towards students with little exposure to programming or computer science topics.
